Idea

Colloquially known as CRIMEWATCH; this website application, primarily designed to analyse historic crime data from the city of Los Angeles, aims to provide significant methods to make user interactible set of solutions for the crime that occurs in this city. Imagine if you moved to the city of L.A. and you do not have a point of reference, The application lets the user recognize patterns in crime, weapons used and the demographics of victims which facilitates better understanding of crime in its entirety
